Privacy Policy for Red Packet Guard

1. Data Collection

To provide security and moderation services, Red Packet Guard processes a limited amount of information:

  • User Identification: We process your Telegram User ID and Username to track warnings, identify administrators, and enforce group-specific rules.
  • Message Analysis: The bot scans message content in real-time to ensure compliance with the 8-10 character alphanumeric rule.

2. Purpose of Processing

The data is used exclusively for:

  • Spam Prevention: Automatically removing messages that do not meet the security criteria.
  • Group Protection: Restricting or banning users who repeatedly violate group policies.
  • Setting Management: Ensuring that the configurations set via the Mini App are applied correctly to the relevant environment.

3. Data Retention and Security

  • No Permanent Storage: Personal messages, media, and private files are not archived or stored on our servers after analysis.
  • Confidentiality: Collected data (such as violation counts) is used only for the bot's internal logic and is never sold, traded, or shared with third-party entities.
  • Administrative Control: All moderation data is tied to the bot's presence in the group; if the bot is removed, its active monitoring and temporary data tracking for that session cease.

4. Required Permissions

For full functionality, the bot requires the following administrative rights:

  • Delete Messages: To clear spam and non-compliant text.
  • Ban/Mute Users: To manage users who compromise the group's security.

5. User Rights

  • Users can avoid data processing by not interacting with the bot or leaving the group where the bot is active.
  • As this is a public moderation tool, continued participation in the group constitutes acceptance of these automated security checks.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to align with Telegram API changes or new bot features. Users are encouraged to review this section regularly.
